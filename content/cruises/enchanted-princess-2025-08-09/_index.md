## Schedule adjustments

There were several changes to the itinerary due to the path of [Hurricane Erin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hurricane_Erin_(2025)):

* Dominica (Day 9, August 17, 2025) was replaced with a stop at Martinique.
* St. Maarten (Day 10, August 18, 2025) was replaced with a stop at St. Kitts.
* The Friday sea day (Day 13, August 22, 2025) was replaced with a stop in Fort Lauderdale, where guests could choose to disembark early or continue to New York.
* Rather than disembarking in New York on Day 14 (August 23, 2025), Day 14 and Day 15 (August 24, 2025) were sea days for the voyage from Fort Lauderdale to New York.
* Disembarkation in New York occurred the morning of Day 16 (Monday, August 25, 2025.)
